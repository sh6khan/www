Money-Converter

When working with systems that heavily depend on FX rates from Yahoo, I realized that if Yahoo had a problem, then my system would break. Money-Converter is a fault tolerant library that looks at many sources and intelligently picks the most accurate rate. It's documented, tested, easy to use, and open source.




Process-Lock

Process-Lock is a fully featured and reliable locking system which ensures that only one instance of your service is opperating at a time. It's used at Coinbase for core services at Coinbase

DBMapper

As an app gets more and more complicated as does your database. You start having too many joins and complex traversals which are very difficult to visualize. Dbmapper is a simple Ruby Gem that helps you see the relationships of all your database tables and information.
